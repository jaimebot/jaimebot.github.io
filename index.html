Jaime is an occasional critic and former art worker from Hong Kong. She was a contributing editor for Spike Art Magazine and 𝚌𝚑𝚊𝚒𝚛𝚙𝚎𝚛𝚜𝚘𝚗 of Chaoyang Trap, a experimental group chat newsletter about contemporary life in China (2021–2023). She currently lives in Hong Kong, where she is working on an essay as the recipient of the 2023 Silvers Foundation Grant. She studied human rights and internatonal law and works to support cross-border litigation for migrant domestic workers in Hong Kong.
